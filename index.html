Why this matters

Not all CPAs speak Aspire.

Aspire owns your job-level revenue and COGS — work tickets, WIP, materials, crew time, the cost of every job. QuickBooks owns everything else — indirect expenses, overhead, payroll allocations, G&A, the full P&L. The controllership work tying the two together — chart of accounts alignment, WIP reconciliation, overhead allocation, work-ticket-to-GL traceability — is where most landscape companies are quietly losing margin. Most bookkeepers and even most CPAs have never touched it.
